About Me

My name is Yang! Some people call me Gilbert or Gilgamesh too. Thanks for visiting my website, where you can find links to interesting projects and my social media accounts as well. I'm currently a freshman studying at MIT. Some of my interests include ping-pong and theatre and cross-country, though I also want to learn to draw anime well (and someday set up a booth at Comiket!) and pick up piano again. My favorite composer has always been Chopin and my current favorite piece is the Kocsis piano arrangement of Rachmaninoff's Vocalise. In the past, I competed in many mathematics and computer science competitions. In the future, I hope to learn more about machine learning and artificial intelligence (and make anime real)! I've also watched a ton of anime and would be happy to talk about any of these subjects! Thanks for reading! Emilia best girl!
